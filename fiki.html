Problem

Service providers need a streamlined way to showcase their services and availability, while consumers require a convenient means of booking appointments and engaging with the providers.

Solution / Value Proposition

Timing revolutionizes the way service-based businesses operate and consumers access services by offering a comprehensive platform that delivers convenience, efficiency, and transparency.

Customer Segments

  • Busy Professionals
  • Emergency or On-Demand Users
  • General Service Providers

Competition

Appointfix: Appointfix is a scheduling app used by professionals in Romania, particularly in the beauty and wellness sector. It provides online booking, reminders, and customer management features.

Advantage Over the Competition

  • User-Friendly Interface: Create an intuitive platform for both service providers and consumers, ensuring ease of use for all users.
  • Security and Data Privacy: Prioritize data security and privacy to build trust with consumers and businesses.
  • Responsive Support: Offer excellent customer support in Romanian and English to address inquiries and provide assistance.

Key Metrics

  • User engagement and satisfaction
  • Conversion Rates
  • Customer Feedback and Ratings

Cost Structure

  • Development and maintenance costs
  • Hosting and server expenses
  • Team salaries and overhead

Revenue Streams

  • Booking Fees
  • Licensing for enterprise usage
  • Advertising for a free version
